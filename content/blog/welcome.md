We just relaunched **AI Launch Space** as a focused directory for AI tools built by indie hackers, founders, and small teams. The old weekly-launch format is gone — instead you can now:

- **Browse** the catalog by category, pricing, and use case
- **Rate** tools you've actually used
- **Comment** to share tips, gotchas, or alternatives
- **Submit** your own AI project — free or featured

We'll use this blog for product updates, picks-of-the-week, and writeups about what's working for indie AI founders. If there's something you'd like us to cover, drop a comment on any project page or reach out via the contact form.

Happy building.
